Tip 1.

When you are mining, make sure that you don't dig straight down. This will increase the likelyhood of you falling into lava and losing all of your progress.

Instead of digging straight down, you should create stairs so that you can see where you're going before you go there.
Tip 2.

Sometimes you want to cook or smelt a large amount of material such as beef to turn into steak or sand to burn into glass. Because you need a furnace and a lot of fuel for this task, being fuel efficient is a very important skill.

You can cook with coal or any other hot or flammable material. Here are the best resources to use: wooden plank, coal, blaze rod, and lava bucket.
Tip 3.

If you want to do a bit more damage when attacking mobs, try doing a critical hit.
While falling, melee attacks deal a critical hit, which deals out 150% of the weapon's damage including potion effects.

This will allow you to kill enemies much faster, therfore decreasing your likelyhood of dying.
